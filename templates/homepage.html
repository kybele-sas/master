About Kybele

Kybele is an organization that develops and provides smart agriculture systems. We collect environmental data from your greenhouse and process the data in order to display on a user friendly, visualized interface.

A smart greenhouse that obey your commands and makes it own decisions if you allow!

Kybele places actuators for roof, air conditioning and climate systems, watering systems, illumination systems so that it makes them controllable for you by your mobile application or web browser. Also, it is able to interpret your data and make predictions if you wish. In Kybele's presence, soil is more fertile, agriculture is easier!
